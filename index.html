About Us

Welcome to E-Guest House, nestled in the heart of Maseru, Lesotho’s vibrant capital. Our guest house offers a warm Basotho welcome and modern comfort, making it perfect for both leisure and business travelers. With stunning views of the Maloti Mountains, we provide a serene retreat after a day of exploration.

Our Rooms

We offer a variety of rooms — from cozy singles for solo travelers to spacious suites for families. All rooms include:

  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi
  • Complimentary breakfast with local and continental options
  • Daily room cleaning
  • En-suite bathrooms with hot showers

Facilities

Our facilities are designed to make your stay enjoyable and stress-free:

  • On-site restaurant serving Basotho cuisine like papa, moroho, and sesotho chicken stew
  • Conference hall for business events
  • Secure parking
  • Swimming pool with a mountain view

Explore Lesotho

Lesotho is known as the "Kingdom in the Sky" for its breathtaking high-altitude landscapes. While staying with us, you can explore:

  • Maletsunyane Falls — one of the highest single-drop waterfalls in Africa
  • Thaba Bosiu — the historic mountain fortress of King Moshoeshoe I
  • Sani Pass — a scenic route connecting Lesotho and South Africa
  • Traditional Basotho blanket markets
